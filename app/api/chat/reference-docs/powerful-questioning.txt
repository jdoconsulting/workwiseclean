Powerful Question Formula
  Ability to ask questions that reveal the information needed for maximum benefit to the relationship and the coachee.
    Common
Powerful
Did that make you angry?
How are you feeling about that situation now?
Are you going to finish by 2?
What needs to happen to finish on time?
Why did you do that?
Where do you go from here?
Do you have a problem with x?
How would you characterize your relationship with x?
Did you start the disagreement?
How did you contribute to the discussion?
